# **Unified Action Plan**

## Key Issues and Conflicts
After reviewing all the department reports, the following key conflicts, gaps, and dependencies have been identified:

### Conflicts
1. **Product Catalog Readiness**:
   - **Tech**: Awaiting finalized product catalog data format and confirmation of product categories for development.
   - **Marketing/Operations**: Concern over the quality, imagery, and descriptions of the seed product data and its readiness for beta.

2. **Feedback Widget Workflow**:
   - **Tech**: Needs clarity on whether feedback messages should trigger notifications/emails or just store data in the database.
   - **Operations**: Requests integration of feedback collection with user actions to gather actionable insights.

3. **Onboarding Process**:
   - **Marketing**: Seeks to enhance onboarding with a checklist, walkthroughs, and email drip campaigns.
   - **Operations**: Requests onboarding event tracking for visibility into architect engagement (e.g., tracking key milestones).

### Gaps
1. **Operational Tooling**:
   - **Admin Product Management**: No admin interface for managing product CRUD, labels, and categories—manual curation (100-150 products) will become unscalable without additional tooling.
   - **Admin Invite Dashboard**: No tool yet to manage invites (e.g., viewing, filtering, resending).

2. **Real-Time Collaboration Features**:
   - **Tech**: The MVP uses polling for comments, which poses potential server scalability concerns. A WebSocket-based implementation may be needed post-beta.

3. **Notifications and Collaboration Features**:
   - **Marketing/Operations**: Missing user engagement features (e.g., collaborator notifications, request availability notifications, user activity dashboards).

4. **Communication of Value Proposition**:
   - **Marketing**: Requires updated landing page, high-quality product descriptions/images, architect testimonials, and clear value messaging to align with the architect-first positioning.

### Dependencies
1. Design:
   - Final wireframes and mockups for onboarding components (welcome checklist, walkthrough) and landing page revamp are needed to implement critical marketing improvements.
   - Wireframes for improved catalog filters and tooltips.

2. Leadership Decisions:
   - Approval of product categories and curated product dataset.
   - Decision on feedback workflow (notification vs. database-only storage) for MVP.
   - Decision on funding for a demo video animation for landing page.

3. Tech Resources:
   - Need to develop and prioritize additional tooling (admin interfaces, dashboards, event tracking), as requested by Operations.
   - Align on feasibility of incorporating onboarding enhancements and real-time updates into Wave 2 of MVP.

## Action Plan  

### **Phase 1: Immediate Priorities (Week 1)**  
#### Goal: Address critical gaps before the MVP beta release to ensure core functionality supports architect-first focus.

1. **Finalize Product Catalog Scope and Dataset**:  
   - **Owner:** Product Management  
   - **Action:** Confirm product categories (tiles, fixtures, lighting, cladding) and finalize seed product list with high-quality images and descriptions.  
   - **Deadline:** Start of Week 2  

2. **Develop Database and Core APIs**:  
   - **Owner:** Tech Department  
   - **Action:** Design database schema, implement invite-token auth, and launch product catalog `products` API. Begin collaboration with Marketing to incorporate finalized product data.  
   - **Deadline:** End of Week 1  

3. **Role-Based Actions**:  
   - **Owner:** Product Management  
   - **Action:** Define interaction permissions for architects, homeowners, and contractors on project boards (e.g., viewing, commenting).  
   - **Deadline:** Start of Week 2  

4. **Design Deliverables**:  
   - **Owner:** Design Team  
   - **Action:** Deliver wireframes for the onboarding checklist, landing page update, and catalog with enhanced filters and tooltips.  
   - **Deadline:** End of Week 1
