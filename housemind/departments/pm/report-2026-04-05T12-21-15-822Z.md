## SYNTHESIZED ACTION PLAN: HOUSEMIND MVP DELIVERY AND BETA LAUNCH

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The MVP design provides a strong start to HouseMind's architect-first, invite-only platform, but there are critical gaps in operational scalability, marketing readiness, and feature clarity. The key focus areas are **catalog readiness**, **clear onboarding flows**, **engagement tracking**, and **admin tooling for operational smoothness**.

This plan prioritizes tasks across all departments to balance immediate delivery needs with long-term scalability. Dependencies, ownership, and unresolved decisions requiring founder input are flagged.

---

### PRIORITIZED TASKS

#### 1. **Catalog Build and Product Data Readiness (P0)**  
The product catalog is central to architect engagement. Quality images, detailed descriptions, and the ability to grow the catalog seamlessly are critical.

#### **Deliverables:**
1. **Finalize Product Dataset**:
   - Curate 100-150 launch-ready products for tiles, fixtures, lighting, and cladding with images, descriptions, and tags.
   - Ensure "Reference" vs "Available" labels, and availability of the Request Availability functionality.
   - **Owner**: **Product Management**  
   - **Dependencies**: Operations to validate scalability; Tech for product database readiness.  
   - **Deadline**: **Week 2**

2. **Admin Product Management Interface**:
   - Build an admin UI for CRUD operations (create, read, update, delete) on products to dynamically manage categories and product statuses (reference/available).  
   - **Owner**: **Tech** (Backend & Frontend teams)  
   - **Dependencies**: PM approval for desired functionality.  
   - **Deadline**: **Wave 2 (Post-Beta)**  

#### **Blocked By Founder Decision**:
- If catalog exceeds 150 products or includes additional categories, prioritize designing advanced tagging/filtering systems. Clarify scope and timeline for scaling.

---

#### 2. **Onboarding and Engagement Tracking (P0)**  
The onboarding process must ensure architects can easily begin creating boards and exploring the product catalog. Features for tracking and improving this experience are essential for the pilot's success metrics.

#### **Deliverables:**
1. **Onboarding Funnel Tracking**:
   - Log key onboarding milestones: account creation, first project/board creation, first board shared with another user. Build a dashboard to track funnel completion rates.  
   - **Owner**: **Tech (Backend, Frontend & Data teams)**  
   - **Dependencies**: Input from Product/Marketing for critical events to measure.  
   - **Deadline**: **Week 4**  

2. **Onboarding Improvements**:
   - **Welcome Checklist (P0)**: Add a post-login page guiding new users to create their first project board and invite collaborators.  
     - **Owner**: **Marketing** (UX copy + wireframes), **Tech** (Frontend implementation).  
     - **Deadline**: **Week 5**
   - **Embedded Walkthrough (P1)**: Script miniature in-app walkthroughs to introduce users to project boards, catalogs, and role-based commenting. Determine feasibility with tools like Appcues or Tourkit (for later iterations).  
     - **Owner**: **Marketing** (draft script), **Tech** (implementation feasibility).  
     - **Deadline**: **Wave 2 (Post-Beta)**  

#### **Blocked By Founder Decision**:
- Approval for use of Appcues/Tourkit or other third-party onboarding tools for embedded walkthrough.

---

#### 3. **Refine Invite Flow and Architect Role Communication (P1)**  
A smooth invite flow and role visibility reinforce the architect’s leadership and ensures a good onboarding experience.

#### **Deliverables**:
1. **Admin Invite Dashboard**:
   - Provide visibility into active invite tokens (sent, accepted, etc.) with the ability to resend or revoke invites.  
   - **Owner**: **Tech (Backend & Frontend teams)**  
   - **Dependencies**: API support for listing and managing invites.  
   - **Deadline**: **Wave 2 (Post-Beta)**  

2. **Architect Role Labeling (UI Enhancements)**:
   - Highlight the primary role of architects in projects with “Lead Architect” labels, clear default permissions, and visual cues (e.g., default "Lead Architect" label on project boards).  
   - **Owner**: **Marketing** (UX copy), **Design** (mockups).  
   - **Dependencies**: Product approval; Tech implementation.  
   - **Deadline**: **Week 3**

---

#### 4. **User Engagement Features: Notifications and Feedback (P1)**
Improve architect and collaborator engagement by introducing lightweight notifications and feedback mechanisms.

#### **Deliverables:**
1. **Notifications for Collaborators**:
   - Notify users when invited to a project, comments are added to a shared board, or products are added to the board.  
   - **Owner**: **Tech (Backend & Frontend teams)**  
   - **Dependencies**: Backend system for triggering notifications.  
   - **Deadline**: **Wave 2 (Post-Beta)**  

2. **Feedback Widget Refinement**:
   - Confirm feedback remains anonymous for the beta phase and structure feedback for analysis by Ops. Decide if feedback should trigger email alerts for administrators.  
   - **Owner**: **Product** (requirements), **Tech** (build).  
   - **Deadline**: **Week 4**

#### **Blocked By Founder Decision**:
- Should notifications trigger emails for critical actions (e.g., shared boards, availability requests) or remain in-app only? Define priorities.

---

#### 5. **Operational Visibility Dashboards (P2)**  
Validate architect and collaborator engagement through real-time tracking and actionable analytics.

#### **Deliverables:**
1. **Onboarding Funnel Dashboard**:
   - Visualize % of users completing onboarding milestones.  
   - **Owner**: **Tech (Frontend, Data)**  
   - **Dependencies**: Backend logging setup from previous task.  
   - **Deadline**: **Wave 5**

2. **User Activity Dashboard**:
   - Track pinned product counts, shared boards, comments, and "Request Availability" actions.  
   - **Owner**: **Tech** (Frontend, Data).  
   - **Dependencies**: Backend logging setup for availability requests and commenting.  
   - **Deadline**: **Wave 5**

### BLOCKED DECISIONS FOR THE FOUNDER
1. **Catalog Scope and Scalability**:
   - Will the catalog scale beyond 150 products/categories? Should prioritization shift to advanced filters and tagging?  

2. **Notifications and Feedback**:
   - Should notifications (e.g., comments on shared boards) trigger emails for collaborators, admins, or remain in-app only?  
   - Should the feedback widget remain anonymous or integrate with user actions for deeper insights?  

3. **Budget for Marketing Enhancements**:
   - Allocate a media production budget for a **landing page demo animation** showcasing HouseMind’s collaborative features.  

---

### CRITICAL DEPENDENCIES
1. **Design**:
   - Deliver final mockups for role-based labeling UI, "Lead Architect" enhancements, Welcome Checklist, and refined feedback UI.  
2. **Tech**:
   - Complete backend event logging for onboarding funnel and "Request Availability."  
3. **Marketing**:
   - Finalize onboarding email drip campaigns and architect-centric outreach copy.  
4. **Ops**:
   - Validate the product dataset and help curate scalable content/tag structures for future phases.

This action plan ensures a cohesive effort across departments to meet MVP success criteria while addressing gaps in engagement, scalability, and operational visibility. Prompt action and founder decisions will be critical to hitting the beta launch milestones.
