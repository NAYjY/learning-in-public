### **Hierarchical Projects Naming and Description**

**Name suggestion:**  
- **Project** → High-level umbrella that represents the entire house build/design plan.  
- **Subproject** → Specific components or areas within your main project, such as “Living Room,” “Kitchen,” or “Outdoor Patio.”

**Description:**  
Keep your project organized by dividing it into manageable subprojects. Each subproject represents a specific area or component of the house design where users can add reference images, annotations, and linked products.
