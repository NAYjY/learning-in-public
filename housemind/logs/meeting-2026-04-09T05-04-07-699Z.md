**"Request Product" button is in scope — it's literally a form and an email, I can build it in 2 hours.**

Here's the implementation:

**This actually makes the product panel MORE valuable:**
- Empty state becomes "No products yet — be the first to request one"
- Architects feel heard immediately (even before Ops responds)
- We get data on what's missing from our catalog
